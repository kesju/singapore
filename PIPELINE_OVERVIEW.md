# Pipeline Overview

## Planning Flow

1. Collect trip basics in `README.md` and `PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`.
2. Add confirmed travel dates, travelers, and base location to `itinerary.md`.
3. Save booking documents in `bookings/`.
4. Track candidate restaurants, attractions, and shopping stops in `places.md`.
5. Estimate expected costs in `budget.md`.
6. Build and refine the packing checklist in `packing.md`.

## Update Loop

1. Add new information as soon as it is confirmed.
2. Move uncertain ideas into notes rather than deleting them immediately.
3. Review budget and itinerary together after major bookings.
4. Keep booking filenames descriptive, such as `flight-outbound.pdf` or `hotel-confirmation.pdf`.

## Completion Criteria

- Travel dates and daily plan are filled in.
- Key bookings are stored in `bookings/`.
- Budget has estimated and actual totals.
- Packing checklist is reviewed before departure.
